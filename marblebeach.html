Marble Beach

Marble Beach is another beautiful beach located about 15.4 km east coast of Trincomalee district. The sea water of the Marble Sea is very clear and the seabed can be seen very clearly which is why this beach is named as the Marble Beach. Swimming is a great beach for little kids to play with. This beach is controlled by SL navy. A very safe beach. The cleanliness is very high.

Traveling Time: 28 min (15.4 km) Trincomalee to Marble Beach

Cost: Free

Best time to visit in year: April to October.

Special Remarks

There is a fee to enter this beach December is not a good month to visit as the sea is a bit rough Parking is available.
